About MatatuHub

MatatuHub is a transport management platform designed to support the daily operations of matatu services. The system enables staff to manage vehicles, payroll, payments and reports in one centralized platform, improving efficiency and accountability.

Routes We Serve

🚐 Thika

Reliable daily transport connecting commuters to the city.

🚐 Rongai

Efficient operations ensuring smooth passenger movement.

🚐 Ngong

Well coordinated routes supported by our fleet management system.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide a reliable digital platform that helps staff manage transport operations efficiently while improving service delivery.

🌍 Our Vision

To become a leading technology-driven transport management organization in the public transport sector.

⭐ Our Motto

"Driving Efficiency, Delivering Reliability."
